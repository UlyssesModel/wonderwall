## Empirical

Pipeline A → C input-token compression of **276×** on the same downstream LLM
(Qwen-2.5-7B). Pipeline C ROUGE-L of 0.116 preserves **~71% of Pipeline A's
quality** (0.163) at a small fraction of the input footprint. Pipeline C beats
Pipeline B on quality (0.116 vs 0.068) — the embedding bridge preserves more
information than the text bridge, exactly as the architectural thesis predicts.

## What this validates

- Wonderwall's projection adapter shape is compatible with Qwen-2.5-7B's
  embedding layer.
- Soft-token injection via `inputs_embeds` produces coherent narrations on
  real GPU silicon.
- The lineage-bridging adapter trains stably.
- `make demo` is reproducible from a fresh clone.

## What this does NOT validate

- Kirk's structural-disorder encoding capability (stub data).
- Regime-classification accuracy (single-regime stub).
- The production 40× cost-AND-quality claim (rests on Uhura's real-Kirk
  leaderboard).
- Cross-domain transfer (single-domain).

All of the above require the real-Kirk eval on `tdx-amx-node-octo` — see
`HANDOFF.md` item 3 for the path.
